PARENTS SQUAD

Pick the Right Squad

Children choose their age group to access age-appropriate challenges and themes. Each squad is designed to match their level of curiosity and learning.

  • Junior (ages 6–10) for play-based learning
  • Teen (ages 11–14) for more advanced scenarios
  • Parents for guidance and oversight

Choose Your Adventure

Kids have different themes to choose from, each packed with exciting challenges and scenarios. From first aid to navigation skills, every theme offers unique survival learning experiences.

  • Rescue Ready - First aid and emergency response
  • Danger Busters - Safety awareness and protection
  • Nature Rangers - Outdoor survival and camping skills
  • Find Your Way - Navigation and orientation

Final Quiz & Checkmarks

Play the games, check them off when you’re done, and unlock the final quiz to prove your survival skills.

  • Checkmark each game as you finish
  • Unlock the final squad quiz
  • Show what you’ve learned
