ABOUT READY BUDDY

Our Adventure Started Here

Every great adventure begins with preparation. Ready Buddy was born from a simple realization: kids do not have all the tools to be ready in any given situation.

We believe that when kids gain survival skills, they're not just ready for emergencies, they're ready for anything life throws their way. That's why we created age-appropriate "squads" that turn essential life skills into engaging adventures.

Ready Buddy transforms preparedness from a chore into an adventure. It will be an exciting journey of discovery, learning, and bonding

What Drives Our Squad

Fun-First Learning

We believe the best learning happens when kids are having fun. Every challenge, game, and activity is designed to be engaging and age-appropriate.

Family Bonding

Emergency preparedness becomes a shared family adventure. Parents and kids learn together, building stronger connections while building essential skills.

Real-World Ready

Every skill we teach has practical application. We focus on building confidence and competence that serves families in everyday situations and emergencies.
