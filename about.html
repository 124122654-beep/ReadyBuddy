ABOUT READY BUDDY

Our Adventure Started Here

Ready Buddy was born from a simple realization: kids don’t always have the tools they need to be prepared for any situation.

We believe that when kids develop essential survival and life skills, they’re not just ready for emergencies, they’re ready for anything life throws their way. That’s why we’ve created age-appropriate “squads” that turn learning into an engaging adventure.

With Ready Buddy, preparedness is no longer a chore, it’s a journey of discovery, learning, and bonding.

What Drives Our Squad

Fun-First Learning

We believe the best learning happens when kids are having fun. Every challenge, game, and activity is designed to be engaging and age-appropriate.

Squad Groups

Our Squad Groups are designed around age-specific adventures, making learning fun and engaging for every stage.

Real-World Ready

Every skill we teach has practical application. We focus on building confidence and competence that serves families in everyday situations and emergencies.
